----- NAME -----
BFS layer sizes of the 3D right-corner orthoscheme reflection group with three pairwise distinct positive perpendicular legs.

----- DATA -----
1, 4, 9, 18, 36, 72, 144, 288, 576, 1152, 2304, 4608, 9216, 18432, 36864

----- OFFSET -----
0,2

----- COMMENTS -----
The 3D right-corner orthoscheme has vertex V_0 with three mutually perpendicular legs of pairwise distinct positive (e.g. rational) lengths a, b, c. W = <R_0, R_1, R_2, R_3> is generated by the reflections across the four faces; a(n) is the number of distinct affine isometries at minimal W-word-length n.

This is the base case (dimension 3) of the Class C family of right-corner orthoschemes.

a(n) equals the coefficients of the exact Coxeter (Steinberg-Solomon) Poincare growth series of this diagram: a path on four nodes whose three consecutive pairs are labelled infinity and whose non-consecutive pairs are perpendicular (m = 2, hence commuting). The realized growth is therefore strictly below the free-Coxeter maximum.

Leg-independence (theorem): for pairwise distinct legs every consecutive dihedral angle is an irrational multiple of Pi (Niven-Conway-Jones), which reduces the relevant non-degeneracy to rank-0 descent on specific elliptic curves; hence the affine representation of W is faithful, the realized growth equals the abstract Coxeter word-growth, and a(n) is a leg-independent closed form.

----- FORMULA -----
a(0) = 1, a(1) = 4, a(2) = 9; a(n) = 2*a(n-1) for n >= 3, so a(n) = 9*2^(n-2) for n >= 2.

G.f.: (1 + t)^2 / (1 - 2*t).

a(n+1)/a(n) -> 2 for n >= 2; 2 = 1 + 2*cos(2*Pi/6) is the n = 3 instance of r_n = 1 + 2*cos(2*Pi/(n+3)).
