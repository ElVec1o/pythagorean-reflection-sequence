----- NAME -----
BFS layer sizes of the 6D right-corner orthoscheme reflection group with six pairwise distinct positive perpendicular legs.

----- DATA -----
1, 7, 27, 82, 226, 597, 1545, 3957, 10080, 25605, 64944, 164592

----- OFFSET -----
0,2

----- COMMENTS -----
The 6D right-corner orthoscheme has vertex V_0 with six mutually perpendicular legs of pairwise distinct positive lengths. W is the reflection group generated by reflections in the seven bounding facets; a(n) is the number of distinct affine isometries of W at minimal word length n (the size of BFS layer n in the Cayley graph).

a(n) equals the coefficients of the exact Coxeter (Steinberg-Solomon) Poincare growth series of this diagram: a path on seven nodes in which consecutive facet pairs are labelled infinity and all non-consecutive pairs are perpendicular (m = 2, commuting). The realized growth is therefore not the free Coxeter maximum, since the perpendicular (commuting) facet pairs reduce the word growth below the free-product bound.

Leg-independence (Class C): for six pairwise distinct legs every consecutive dihedral angle is an irrational multiple of Pi (Niven-Conway-Jones), which reduces the relevant relation to a rank-0 descent on specific elliptic curves; hence the affine representation is faithful, the realized growth equals the abstract Coxeter word-growth, and a(n) is a leg-independent closed form. This is a theorem (Bonfioli 2026, Class C), not a conjecture. Verified by exact-rational BFS on legs (1, 2, 3, 5, 7, 11) through depth 8 and by power-series expansion of the generating function.

----- FORMULA -----
G.f.: (1 + t)^4 / (1 - 3*t + 3*t^3).

a(n) = 3*a(n-1) - 3*a(n-3) for n >= 5.

a(n+1)/a(n) -> 1 + 2*cos(2*Pi/9) = 2.5320888..., the largest real root of x^3 - 3*x^2 + 3 (the reverse polynomial of the g.f. denominator 1 - 3*t + 3*t^3). This is the n = 6 case of r_n = 1 + 2*cos(2*Pi/(n+3)).
