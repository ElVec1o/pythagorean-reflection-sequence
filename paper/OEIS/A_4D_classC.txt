%N BFS layer sizes of the 4D right-corner orthoscheme reflection group with four pairwise distinct positive perpendicular legs.

%C The 4D right-corner orthoscheme has vertex V_0 with four mutually perpendicular legs of pairwise distinct positive lengths. W is the reflection group on the five bounding faces; a(n) is the number of distinct affine isometries at minimal W-word-length n.

%C a(n) equals the coefficients of the exact Coxeter (Steinberg-Solomon) Poincaré growth series of this diagram: a path on the five generators in which consecutive pairs are labelled infinity and non-consecutive pairs are perpendicular (m = 2, commuting). The realized growth is not the free-Coxeter maximum, since the perpendicular facet pairs commute.

%C Leg-independence (Class C): for pairwise distinct legs every consecutive dihedral angle is an irrational multiple of pi (Niven-Conway-Jones), which reduces faithfulness to rank-0 descent on specific elliptic curves; hence the affine representation is faithful, the realized growth equals the abstract Coxeter word-growth, and a(n) is a leg-independent closed form. This is proven (Bonfioli 2026), not conjectural.

%F G.f.: (1 + t)^3 / (1 - 2 t - t^2 + t^3).

%F Linear recurrence: a(n) = 2 a(n-1) + a(n-2) - a(n-3) for n >= 4.

%F Asymptotic ratio a(n+1)/a(n) -> 1 + 2 cos(2 pi / 7) = 2.2469796..., the largest real root of x^3 - 2 x^2 - x + 1 (the reciprocal polynomial of the g.f. denominator 1 - 2 t - t^2 + t^3).
